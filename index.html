How to buy $AMETHYST
To buy the $AMETHYST token on Raydium, first install a BNB wallet like Phantom and add some SOL to cover transaction fees. Go to Raydium, connect your wallet, and navigate to the Swap section. Select SOL to trade, search for $AMETHYST (or add its contract address if needed), enter the amount, and confirm the transaction. $AMETHYST will appear in your wallet after the swap is complete.
